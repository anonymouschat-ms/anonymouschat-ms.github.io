Our Commitment

  • We maintain a zero-tolerance policy towards any content, behavior, or conduct that sexualizes or exploits minors.
  • We do not allow the creation, distribution, or promotion of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM/CSEM).
  • We actively enforce our policies through a combination of automated tools and human moderation.

What We Prohibit

  • Images, videos, text, or other media that depict sexualized minors or sexual acts involving minors.
  • Requests, offers, instructions, or facilitation of sexual activity involving minors.
  • Any content that attempts to exploit, groom, or recruit minors.

Reporting and Enforcement

If you encounter content or behavior that you believe violates these standards, please report it immediately. When reporting, provide as much information as possible (usernames, timestamps, screenshots, links).

How We Respond

  • Reports are triaged and investigated promptly by our safety team.
  • We remove violating content and, when appropriate, suspend or terminate offending accounts.

Prevention & Safety Measures

  • Automated detection systems to identify and block CSAM and grooming behavior.
  • Human moderation and manual review for escalated or ambiguous cases.
  • Age-appropriate design principles and safety-by-default settings where applicable.
  • Regular staff training on child protection best practices and legal obligations.

Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable laws relating to child protection and will report illegal content to the relevant authorities. We follow recognized industry guidance, including Google Play’s Child Safety policies.
